## How to Determine Which License Applies

1. Check the file header for copyright information
2. HHMI copyright → Janelia License (license_details/LICENSE-JANELIA.txt)
3. Stochastic Analytics copyright → Academic users use MPL-2.0; Commercial users contact for licensing

## Definitions

**Academic Use**: Use by accredited educational institutions, government research
laboratories, and non-profit research organizations for non-commercial research and
educational purposes.

**Commercial Use**: Any use by a for-profit entity, or any use directed toward
commercial advantage or monetary compensation, including but not limited to:

- Integration into commercial products or services
- Use in providing services to third parties for a fee
- Internal use within a commercial entity to support business operations
